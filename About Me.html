About Me

Hi, Welcome! I'm Enes Adil ERTÜRK!

If I’m not mistaken, my interest in Cartoon & Animation and Graphic Design has been growing since I was in seventh grade. I actually have this quiet thing I adore: learning, experiencing. It brings me such joy to create characters and worlds inspired by my life and my friends.

My greatest — and perhaps the saddest — story is about my main character, the one I’ve been dreaming of and drawing for so long now. I just love him. Even in my darkest, most silent moments, I thought of him. And to this day, I continue to pour pieces of my life into his world. Maybe one day, I will have to close this chapter and finally let him go. His name is Morganite.

My greatest ambition in this industry is not only to succeed but to prove myself as a team player. I believe that in animation, teamwork isn’t just a requirement — it’s the doorway to a world where everyone can share their art.

Morganite’s ideal world is a place he calls the “Lovely Lands.”

“Where I get to hold hands, and where I get lost in wonderlands.”

He must outrun the harsh truths of his universe — a world set ten thousand years into the future, where technology, and everything that once gleamed with youth and warmth, has been forbidden.

And for the sake of the damaged generations,
Those who bellow in riot against that grim world,
Those who tremble at the approach of vile men,
He vowed never to surrender.
